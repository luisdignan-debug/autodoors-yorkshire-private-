# Support Checklist

## Weekly

- Check **System** has no red warnings.
- Check mailbox sync still works.
- Download an export.
- Review jobs at risk.
- Review money to collect.
- Review supplier invoices due.

## Monthly

- Export all data.
- Check invoice numbering.
- Check outstanding balances.
- Check supplier invoices and payments.
- Review any stale leads.
- Confirm the client still understands the Today workflow.

## Do Not Promise

- statutory accounting;
- VAT filing;
- Making Tax Digital;
- payroll;
- CIS;
- bank reconciliation;
- legal advice;
- guaranteed lead conversion.

The product helps the business operate cleanly. It does not replace professional accounting.
