# Client Onboarding

## Ten-Minute Walkthrough

1. Show **Dashboard**: what is happening and where money is.
2. Show **Today**: what needs doing first.
3. Open one job: follow the single next action.
4. Show **Finance**: customer balance and supplier owed.
5. Show **Invoices**: create draft, generate PDF, preview email.
6. Show **Supplier Emails**: link or archive supplier messages.
7. Show **Technician Schedule**: daily preview and calendar export.
8. Show **System**: export data and health warnings.
9. Show **Setup**: what remains before full rollout.

## Buyer Promise

One dashboard that tells you what to do today, what money is owed, what supplier orders are stuck, what jobs need installing, and what customers need chasing.

## First Live Test

Use one real lead from start to finish before switching every job into the system.
